Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how SafeCore handles information when you use the SafeCore mobile application, website, and related support features. SafeCore is designed to help people review device security posture, identify privacy risks, and activate protection tools with as little unnecessary data collection as possible.

1. What SafeCore Does

SafeCore is a security-focused mobile utility. Depending on the features you use, the app may review device settings, identify security gaps, surface privacy recommendations, check update status, and help you enable protective tools such as monitoring, secure browsing, VPN-related features, or breach alerts.

2. Information We Process

SafeCore limits processing to information needed to provide app features, improve reliability, and answer support requests. That may include:

  • Device and app signals, such as app version, operating system version, language, and high-level diagnostics.
  • Security-related status data generated by the app, such as whether certain protections are enabled or whether setup is complete.
  • Support information you choose to send to us through forms or customer support.
  • Subscription and transaction status supplied by the platform that manages your purchase.

3. Data You Choose To Provide

Some SafeCore features depend on information you actively enter or confirm. For example, if you contact support or submit a refund request, you may provide your name, email address, subscription details, or message content. We use that information only to respond to your request and manage support operations.

4. Device Permissions

SafeCore may request limited device permissions where a feature requires them. Permission prompts are controlled by your device, and you can deny or revoke access at any time from system settings.

  • Notification permission may be used for security reminders, setup prompts, or important service alerts.
  • Network access is used to load app resources, verify subscription state, connect to support, or check remote security data sources.
  • Other permissions, if shown in the app, are used only for the specific feature that requested them.

5. How We Use Information

  • Deliver, personalize, and maintain SafeCore features.
  • Show your setup state and security recommendations.
  • Process support, billing, and account-related requests.
  • Detect abuse, fraud, technical failures, or service misuse.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and feature quality over time.

6. What We Do Not Sell

SafeCore does not sell your personal information. We do not build advertising profiles around your use of the app, and we do not share personal data with third parties for unrelated marketing campaigns.

7. Third-Party Services

SafeCore may rely on third-party providers for hosting, customer support, subscription processing, analytics, or security lookups. Those providers receive only the information reasonably needed to perform their role and are expected to protect it under their own privacy and security obligations.

8. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed for legitimate business or legal reasons, such as providing the service, handling support, resolving disputes, enforcing agreements, or complying with legal obligations. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and the context in which it was collected.

9. Security Measures

SafeCore uses administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect data against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to reduce risk and limit access to data on a need-to-know basis.

10. Children’s Privacy

SafeCore is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will review the request.

11. Your Choices

  • You can stop using SafeCore at any time.
  • You can revoke device permissions from your phone settings.
  • You can contact support to request help regarding data, billing, or service questions.

12. Updates To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect product changes, legal requirements, or operational updates. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and update the effective date above.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or SafeCore’s privacy practices, contact us through the form on the homepage at Get in Touch.
